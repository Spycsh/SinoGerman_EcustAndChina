East China University of Science and Technology

  • East China University of Science and Technology, referred to as “Huali”, is located in Shanghai and is a science and engineering feature directly under the Ministry of Education of the People’s Republic of China. It covers science, engineering, agriculture, medicine, economics, management, and literature. , research, law, art, philosophy, education, research-oriented national key universities; the country’s first batch of world-class disciplines to build colleges and universities, the country’s first “211 projects”, “985 engineering superiority discipline innovation platform” key construction universities, high level Members of the University’s High-Quality Resource Sharing Alliance, selected as a national high-level university public graduate program, 111 program, excellent engineer education training plan, 2011 plan, new engineering research and practice project, national university student innovative experiment plan, national deepening innovation and entrepreneurship education Reforming model universities, Chinese government scholarships for Chinese students to receive institutions, etc.; 1956, the first batch of schools in China to recruit graduate students, the first batch of doctoral, master’s and bachelor degree units in the country, the first batch of 22 universities in the country to implement independent enrollment reform One of the first six countries has a national technology transfer Innovative center, one of comprehensive research university. In 2018, the school ranked 26th among Chinese universities in the QS World University Rankings and the Times Higher Education World University Rankings; ranked 35th among Chinese universities in the USNews World University Rankings; In the ESI China University Rankings, it is ranked 28th.

  • East China University of Science and Technology, formerly known as East China Institute of Chemical Technology, can be traced back to Nanyang College and Aurora College more than 100 years ago. In October 1952, the Department of Chemical Engineering, Jiaotong University, Aurora University, Datong University, Soochow University, Jiangnan University, etc. The merger was formed. It was more famous in 1993. In 1997, Shanghai participated in the joint construction and joint management.

  • As of December 2018, the school has three parts: Xuhui Campus, Fengxian Campus and Jinshan Science and Technology Park. It covers an area of ​​2,535 acres and a building area of ​​920,000 square meters.
